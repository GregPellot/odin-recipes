Lasagna

Authentic lasagna, rich, cheesy and very filling.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz uncooked lasagna noodles
  • 1 can spaghetti sauce
  • 1 pound cottage cheese
  • 8 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  3. Blend tomato sauce and cottage cheese until smooth. Spoon a little in the bottom of a 9x13 baking dish. Place a layer of cooked noodles over the sauce and sprinkle a portion of the mozzarella, the cheddar and the Parmesan over the noodles. Repeat layering of sauce, noodles and cheese; finish with a cheese layer.
  4. Bake in preheated oven 30 to 45 minutes, until cheese is bubbly and golden.
