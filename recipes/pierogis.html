Pierogis

Ingredients:

  • Mrs. T's Pierogis
  • Aluminum foil
  • Cooking spray

Steps:

  1. Line foil with some cooking spray
  2. Place pierogis in the airfryer, lightly coat them with cooking spray
  3. Fry them at 400 degrees for 12 mins, flip halfway through
  4. Let stand 2 minutes. Recommendation: Enjoy with Bertolli five cheese sauce
